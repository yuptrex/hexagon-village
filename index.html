Walk the Village — Third Person

Desktop: WASD to move, Shift to run, Space to jump, C to crouch, Z to go prone, X to slide. Drag anywhere with the mouse to orbit the camera around your knight.

Fly mode: Press V (or tap FLY) to toggle free flight, Minecraft creative-style. While flying: move with WASD, look up/down with the camera to aim your flight path, Space to rise, C to descend, and Shift for a speed boost. Press V again to land and gravity takes back over.

Mobile: Left joystick to move, drag the right side of the screen to orbit the camera. The bottom-right ring has JUMP, WEAPON-switch and ATTACK. Tap MORE above it for Run, Fly, Crouch, Prone, Slide and Fly-Down.

Combat: Cycle between Sword & Shield, Bow and Axe & Shield with WEAPON (or Q on desktop). Tap ATTACK (click / E) to swing your melee weapon or fire an arrow. Landing hits on the wandering barbarians and rogues staggers and eventually defeats them — they respawn back home after a short while.

Infinite world: Both the grass hex-field and the village itself keep expanding forever as you walk — new homes, towers, wells, trees and rocks generate procedurally around you in every direction.

Note: this KayKit Adventurers pack only ships Idle/Walk/Run/Jump clips — crouch, prone and slide are built as a lean-and-lower pose blended on top of those animations, not native mocap clips.
